Cambodian Temple Complex

    Cambodian Temple Complex was a project I did to test my Spline Architect plugin

    The environment is built using modular meshes and thanks to Spline Architect, its all non-destructive, meaning I can modify the size and layout of the buildings at any time without needing to create specific meshes outside of UE5.
